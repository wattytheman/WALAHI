Our Story

Baked with passion since 2020

Made With Love

Sweet Crumb started in our tiny kitchen with a simple dream - to bring the warmth of homemade baking to our neighborhood.

We use only the finest ingredients - real butter, farm fresh eggs, and locally sourced fruits. No shortcuts, no preservatives, just honest baking.

Every item is baked fresh daily so you always get that perfect warm-from-the-oven experience.
